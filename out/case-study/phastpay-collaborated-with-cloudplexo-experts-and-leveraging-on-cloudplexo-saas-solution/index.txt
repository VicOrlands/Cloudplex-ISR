**PhastPay** is a leading fintech company in Nigeria dedicated to providing digital payment solutions for businesses and individuals. Its user-friendly and secure platform simplifies financial transactions, enabling merchants to accept payments through various channels efficiently.

With a focus on financial inclusion, PhastPay extends its services to underserved communities, empowering them with accessible and affordable digital payment solutions. By driving the adoption of digital payments, PhastPay contributes significantly to economic growth, financial inclusion, and modernization in the financial sector.

## Customer Challenge

As PhastPay’s customer base and digital payment offerings grew, they faced two critical challenges with their AWS infrastructure:

1. **Security Concerns**
   Protecting sensitive financial data and mitigating cybersecurity threats were paramount as the platform scaled.
2. **Cost Optimization Needs**
   Efficiently utilizing resources and maximizing return on investment required a detailed analysis of AWS expenditures to eliminate inefficiencies.

## Solution

PhastPay partnered with **CloudPlexo**, utilizing their **Wendu platform** to address these challenges. Wendu, a SaaS solution for AWS security and cost management, provided a comprehensive framework for enhancing security and optimizing costs.

### Key Steps

1. **Security Assessment**
   * Conducted a detailed evaluation of PhastPay’s AWS infrastructure.
   * Identified vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and potential threats.
   * Implemented best practices and advanced tools to fortify security.
2. **Cost Optimization Analysis**
   * Analyzed resource usage and identified inefficiencies.
   * Recommended rightsizing instances and optimizing storage solutions.
   * Suggested strategies to maintain performance and reliability while minimizing expenses.

* Strengthened defenses against cyber threats.

* Eliminated unnecessary expenses.
* Optimized AWS resource usage.

* Gained the ability to dynamically scale infrastructure resources.
* Positioned for rapid deployment of new services.
